## Observations
- Top page: `/` had 8 views / 4 users per 28 days (last reliable snapshot, 2026-05-24).

- Root cause 1: production was stale — `agent/seo-i18n-hreflang` work (2026-06-08)

  query-param based.
- Root cause 2: even at HEAD, homepage hreflang/canonical collapsed — all 10

  drops the query string when normalizing the root path's trailing slash).

  every locale's canonical at the English page, telling Google all locale
  versions are duplicates while hreflang claimed they were alternates.
- Root cause 4: sitemap listed ~5,000 `?locale=` URLs that canonicalize away
  (duplicate-submission noise).

## Decision
- Today's task: make en/ja/pt/ar the only indexable locale URLs, each with a
  self-referencing canonical and a consistent 4-locale hreflang set; remove all
  query-param locale URLs from the sitemap; deploy and verify on the live site.
- Reason: zero indexing is the binding constraint; nothing downstream
  (CVR, ads) matters until pages can be indexed.
- Expected metric: GSC indexed pages and impressions (see Launch-Phase KPI Gate
  in `AGENTS.md`).
